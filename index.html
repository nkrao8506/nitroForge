Our Identity

Forged in Fire

Nitro Forge Racing Team is a student-led Formula Student team founded with the vision of creating a world-class engineering platform where innovation, manufacturing excellence, and motorsport passion converge into something extraordinary.

As a first-generation Formula Student team from JNTUH UCESTH, we are building our foundation from the ground up. Every component, every design decision, every manufacturing process — forged with purpose. The car is not merely built. It is forged.

Our mission is not simply to build a race car. Our mission is to forge engineers capable of solving real-world challenges through design, teamwork, manufacturing, and innovation.

The Competition

Formula Bharat 2027

Formula Bharat is India's premier Formula Student competition where engineering students design, manufacture, validate, and race a formula-style race car — competing across both static and dynamic events that test every aspect of the vehicle and the team behind it.

⬡ Static Event
Engineering Design
Judges evaluate the technical merit and innovation of the vehicle design against the rules and engineering best practices.
⬡ Static Event
Cost & Manufacturing
Teams present a detailed cost report demonstrating real-world manufacturing awareness and economic considerations.
⬡ Static Event
Business Presentation
A business case for producing and selling the vehicle as a marketable product to a fictitious manufacturer.
◆ Dynamic Event
Acceleration
Straight-line sprint over 75 meters from a standing start — pure powertrain performance.
◆ Dynamic Event
Skid Pad & Autocross
Circular handling test and a tight technical course evaluating lateral dynamics and driver skill.
◆ Dynamic Event
Endurance & Efficiency
The flagship event — a 22-kilometre race that tests reliability, driver performance, and fuel efficiency over time.

Technical Overview

Design & Engineering

🔩
Chassis System
✦ Design Completed
Safety-first frame engineered for structural strength and driver protection, fully compliant with Formula Bharat regulations. Steel space-frame architecture optimised for torsional rigidity.
⚙️
Powertrain
● In Development
Combustion engine integration focused on peak performance, torque delivery, and long-run reliability. Drivetrain layout optimised for the endurance event.
🔧
Suspension
● In Development
Double-wishbone setup designed for precise handling and maximum driver feedback. Geometry validated through kinematic simulation for all competition events.
🌬️
Aerodynamics
◌ Concept Stage
Front wing and diffuser concept under development targeting meaningful downforce gains within the weight and cost constraints of a first-year build.
💻
Electronics
◌ Development Stage
Data acquisition system, sensor integration, and real-time vehicle health monitoring to support driver performance analysis across all dynamic events.
🏗️
Manufacturing
◌ Preparation Phase
Process planning, tooling procurement, and workshop preparation underway. The team is equipping itself for precision fabrication of all structural and mechanical components.
2026–27 Season

Development Journey

Phase 01
Team Formation
Core team assembly and department structure establishment. Selecting motivated engineers across all subsystems.
Phase 02
Formula Bharat Registration
Official team registration, rulebook study, and competition preparation. Understanding the technical regulations fully.
Phase 03
Vehicle Concept & Chassis Design
Complete vehicle concept established. Chassis design finalised and ready for detailed analysis.
Phase 04
CAD Development & Simulation
Full vehicle CAD model in progress. FEA, CFD, and kinematic simulations across all subsystems ongoing.
Phase 05
Design Reviews & Validation
Internal design reviews and external mentor reviews. Incorporating feedback before committing to manufacturing.
Phase 06
Manufacturing & Fabrication
NF-01 physical build begins. Chassis fabrication, component manufacturing, and system integration.
Phase 07
Testing & Validation
Shake-down testing, system validation, and driver training. Preparing for competition-ready reliability.
